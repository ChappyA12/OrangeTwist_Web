About Us

Mary Asel and Andrea Roberson started Orange Twist in 2009. These friends and neighbors originally met at a kindergarten bus stop but really started to have fun when they discovered their complementary skills while partnering on a fundraiser event.

So began the "Where did you get that?" phenomenon. When Mary moved overseas, she started scouring local markets to bring back gifts for friends. Andrea sensed a great opportunity. Together they set to work on building a business bringing pieces state-side and into beautiful boutiques as unique and fun as the jewelry.

Mary's and Andrea's experience in International Business Planning and media-related marketing, and their gratitude for the every day joys of life, with some bling on it of course, made for an exciting little jewelry company "that could."

Today thanks to an outstanding group of sales reps, Orange Twist jewelry is sold a wide variety of fabulous boutiques and fine museum stores around the corner and across the country. We have also proudly designed pieces for sale in a number of catalogs.
